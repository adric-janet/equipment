What is equipment.data.ac.uk

An EPSRC project funded to deliver a sustainable “national equipment portal” for the HE sector (Joint ESPRC/Jisc funded from 1st April 15). The service provides an aggregation of all published UK HE equipment datasets – making it the “one stop shop” for facilitating equipment collaboration discussions
