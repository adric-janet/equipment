How are you contributing?

With ease of contribution being a major success criteria for equipment.data ,it is unsurprising that most contributors have chosen the easiest route! You may be surprised to note this method is the humble Excel spreadsheet, either manually completed, or generated by your asset register and published to a webpage. A whopping 38% use this method of publishing data. With conversations turning to other datasets, interoperability and simple methods of data sharing will be critical. Observers would be wise to take lessons from this preference.
