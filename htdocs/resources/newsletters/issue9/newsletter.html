Making publishing your data easy – Our new step by step guides

Our new guides describe the three easy steps to publishing your data along with explaining the role of the OPD in making your data auto-discoverable. Not only will these guides help you with publishing your research equipment availability, they will also make data describing the wider activity of your institution accessible to new search engines and data services – including the National Centre for Universities and Business’ (NCUB) ‘Intelligent Brokerage Tool’ and Jisc Research Data Discovery Service (RDDS) currently under development.

How are you contributing?

With ease of contribution being a major success criteria for equipment.data ,it is unsurprising that most contributors have chosen the easiest route! You may be surprised to note this method is the humble Excel spreadsheet, either manually completed, or generated by your asset register and published to a webpage. A whopping 38% use this method of publishing data. With conversations turning to other datasets, interoperability and simple methods of data sharing will be critical. Observers would be wise to take lessons from this preference. Read more
