The OPD - enabling greater efficiency in data discovery

With many organisations now establishing an Organisational Profile Document (OPD) enabling auto-discovery of their equipment data, opportunities for greater efficiency in data discovery is possible (Read more)

The importance of asset management

A fundamental challenge that has arisen from the development of equipment databases has been the issues it has raised in asset management resulting in the need for improved and enhanced data along with interoperability with other institutional systems (Read more)

Achieving efficiencies through partnership

We have recently started working with APUC to discuss the opportunities and benefits of an Application Programming Interface (API) feature enabling publication of user selected capital items from the EDAM database in the UNIQUIP data publishing specification format. (Read more)
