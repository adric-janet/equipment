N8 launch Equipment Sharing Toolkit

Earlier this month the N8 launched its Equipment Sharing Toolkit (EST). Aimed at addressing operational issues that can arise when university research equipment is shared, the toolkit comprises a set of guiding principles and templates covering four key areas: health and safety, pricing and charging, contracts and legal and VAT.
