Would you be interested in our webinar?

From where you capture your institutions equipment data to how to share with the national portal – we’re holding a webinar that will break down in the simplest terms how to get your equipment and facilities data on to the national portal and beyond.

Our webinar will take you through the step-by-step journey from capturing your equipment and facilities data, identifying the most suitable system to manage it, complying with the publishing specification, making it discoverable, licensing it, contributing to the national portal and then maintaining it for the future.

If you would be keen to participate in the lunchtime webinar from the comfort of your own desk, please email feedback@data.ac.uk to register your interest.
