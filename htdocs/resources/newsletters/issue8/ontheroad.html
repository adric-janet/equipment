equipment.data on the road

International Open Data Conference 2015 (IODC15) 26-29th May

  • Adrian Cox, our Project Manager, delivered a well-received presentation explaining the equipment.data development, its application as a national equipment search service and the challenges for the adoption and wider use of the technology delivering the service.
  • the plenary sessions highlighted the increased interest from national governments in delivering open data with 72 of the 76 countries now engaged in the open government partnership represented. http://www.opengovpartnership.org/

ARMA Annual Conference 3-4th June

    equipment.data exhibited at this year’s conference.... thanks to all those who said “hello”. A number of new contacts were made with many institutions keen to learn more about the service.
  • This year our poster highlighted the infrastructure equipment.data has established and how this can contribute to the growing demand for data discovery - Poster
  • We had cause for celebration with Adrian being recognised with the “Highly commended” award under the category Technology, Innovation and application. The award recognised the hard work put into engaging with institutions across the UK which has established equipment.data the recognition it has as the “national equipment and facilities portal”.
